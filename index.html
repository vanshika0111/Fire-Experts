About

The Management:

Fire Experts is entrusted to highly qualified and experienced, enthusiastic, trained technical professionals from the aforesaid industries. The management of the group comprises personalities from different fields including Engineering, Business Management and Cost Accountancy having hands on experience in renowned Indian companies. The nature of business is exporter, importer, supplier & installer. We cover domestic and international markets with the annual turnover of INR 45,000,000+.

Our Motto:

Our mission is to give our customers safety with peace of mind, which is delivered through in-depth knowledge of customer applications and best of world class services through association with different experienced experts.

Our Vision

To become an internationally acclaimed Fire Safety services provider Company giving Total Engineering Solutions to the entire Satisfaction of the Customers.
